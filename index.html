Genmega 2500

Barbershops, events and convenience stores

The Genmega G2500 ATM machine is preferred by customers over competitors due to its professional and bank-like appearance. It is designed for retail and off-premise financial institution locations and offers a variety of hardware configuration choices. The machine is user-friendly, visually appealing, and considered one of the best machines from a service standpoint.

Genmega Onyx

Hotels, Restaurants and Bars

The Genmega Onyx ATM is a budget-friendly, high-end retail ATM designed to make a statement and encourage usage. Its compact, sleek, and sophisticated design, along with its dark gunmetal paint, black bezel, and large LCD display, make it popular in fancy hotel lobbies and nightclubs. The ATM is available in various dispenser configurations and printer options, making it highly customizable to fit different needs and locations.

Genmega C6000

Gentlemen's Clubs

The Genmega C6000 ATM machine offers a variety of options for every budget and application, including a Level One rated safe, presenter style cash dispenser, and touch screen. Its standout feature is a 15" standard display, which can be upgraded to a touch screen. The ATM is popular in high-end salons, nightclubs, casinos, poker rooms, and convenience stores, and is frequently used for off-premise financial branded ATMs. Online pricing is inclusive of a Best Products ATM processing discount, and additional fees may apply for installation in remote regions.
